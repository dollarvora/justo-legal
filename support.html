Justo Support

Last updated: February 2026

Contact Us

For questions, feedback, or issues, email us at support@justo-app.com. We aim to respond within 48 hours.

Account Issues

  • Can't log in? Try resetting your password from the login screen. If you still have trouble, email us.
  • Delete my account: Go to Settings > Delete My Account. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
  • Export my data: Go to Settings > Export My Data to download a copy of all your information.

Reporting Prices

  • Only report prices you personally observed.
  • Include the venue name and item for accurate comparisons.
  • Photos are optional but help the community — faces are automatically blurred.

Privacy & Terms

Read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for full details on how we handle your data and what we expect from users.

Emergency Numbers

Justo is NOT an emergency service. If you are in danger, contact local authorities:

  • 190 — Police (Polícia Militar)
  • 192 — Ambulance (SAMU)
  • 193 — Fire Department (Bombeiros)
